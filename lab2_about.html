I chose food waste as a subject because from a very young age, it has always been one of the good values instilled in me by my parents. In my opinion it is very important to reduce the waste of food because it helps first to prevent our environment. I grew up in a community where the children or families did not have food to eat sometimes throughout the day and I often saw my mother going to distribute food to street children who were hungry, including widows. abandoned by their families. If we made our food plans well at home, we could give the surplus food to people who are hungry and this would be a double advantage because not only will we be helping people to feed themselves but also we will reduce/prevent the waste of food. food.
